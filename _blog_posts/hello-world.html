It began when I saw Jesse Eisenberg blogging in The Social Network. Although not as risqu√©, I decided my blog would mostly be a creative outlet about the projects I have built or the books I've read.

But here we are in 2019, and I haven't started one yet.

But halfway through the year I had a sudden burst of inspiration and decided to restore my personal website. I figured in the process I'd probably learn a few things about what's new in web development and brush up my css-craft and js-fu.

As I began desiging my website, I realized that having an outlet to express myself felt really good. I wanted to document the though process which went into desiging it (more on that later). This lead to a ground-breaking realization: what better way to document it than *gasp* writing a blog.

So here is my very first blog post to commemorate what is hopefully going to be a regular habit. I'll try to blog about my projects, books I found interesting and other general ramblings.
